About Us

Learn more about our mission and the team behind FoodShare.

Our Mission

FoodShare was founded in 2019 with a simple yet powerful mission: to bridge the gap between food surplus and food scarcity. We believe that no one should go hungry when perfectly good food goes to waste.

Through our network of dedicated volunteers and generous donors, we collect surplus food from restaurants, events, households, and businesses, and distribute it to families and communities in need.

Our technology-driven approach ensures efficient pickup and delivery, minimizing food waste while maximizing impact. Every donation, big or small, helps us move closer to a world without hunger.
